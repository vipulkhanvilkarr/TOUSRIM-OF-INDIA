A kaleidoscope of traditions, culture, and vibrant geographies, India speaks for itself as a soul-stirring journey. Snow covered peaks of Himalayas to stretch of coastline, natural greenery to depths of spirituality and clusters of cultural shades. All of these aspects join together to define the raw beauty through which India captures the heart of every tourist. Discover the different facets of this multicolored country as it shapes your vision at every of its fold. With the country's tourism branched into several forms, India has a chunk for every kind of a traveler. To get things going, here is an India travel planner which incorporates must-visit tourist attractions, places to visit in India, how to reach and the best time to visit.While any holiday in India is sure to grant you memories of a lifetime, there are some of the most exciting categories that tempt you to make plans for India tour at the earliest hour

The path to healing and enlightenment leads to India: The essence, roots and everything healing is about, lies deep within India. Practiced in the form of Ayurveda, Yoga, and meditation, all these forms bring out the best in you. While Ayurveda, the system of medicine has been bestowed from Gods to Sages to human physicians for over 5000 years now. Kerala, Goa, Uttarakhand, Udaipur, Karnataka run in the influence of Ayurveda. Yoga originated in Ancient India, and with just a little practice of the postures helps you bring a lot of positive changes in your body. Come down to Rishikesh, the Yoga Capital of the World and a spot holder of UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage. Bihar, Goa, Karnataka, Uttarakhand, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh are just the right place to practice Yoga. On the other hand, meditation enlightens the mind and paves a way for the person into a more stable and calm state. Pondicherry, Kerala, Bengaluru, Pune, Chennai, Rishikesh, Delhi are a haven for meditators. Yoga and meditation holiday packages in India are for the ones who want to unlock the secrets of ancient Indian wellness.

Exchange your sunny days with breezy happiness at hill stations: Just the idea of being surrounded by the mighty mountains, snow cap on or off, is a dream. Which is why going for a vacation to hill stations in India is a blissful experience. That being said, there are innumerable destinations from north to south and east to west that quench your thirst for a perfect escape from summers. From trying adventure activities, sitting in solace, feeling the joy of shopping, meeting wild creatures, India has a lot in store for its travelers. Srinagar, Nainital, Manali, Shimla, Mussoorie, Ooty, Munnar, Matheran, Kodaikanal are some top picks.

Exploring the wild: Is your camera and focus on point?: India possesses a striking diversity when it comes to wildlife. Often called the other half, the wild creatures have their presence marked in different states of the nation. With over 400 wildlife sanctuaries, 89 national parks, and 13 bio-reserves, India is best known for having the richest biodiversity in the world. Take your anticipation a level up at Kaziranga National Park, Assam; Periyar National Park, Kerala; Sasan Gir National Park, Corbett, Ranthambore, and Bandhavgarh. These are some of the top national parks in India where thrill and adventure run hand in hand. Royal Bengal Tigers, Indian elephants, Asiatic Lions and 2/3rd of Great Single-horned Rhinoceros are the significant species that adds one reason for travelers to visit India. Along with them, the motherland sings a lullaby to 400 species of mammals, 1250 birds, 2550 fishes, 460 reptiles, 240 amphibians, and 2550 fishes. Tiger safari in India is a rare thrill. Wildlife safari tours are among the most sought options of India travel as the heart of nature is kept intact and even preserved.

Stare at two different views, one your partner and other being the honeymoon destination in India you choose. Pamper yourself at luxurious hotels, spot sunrise, and sunsets, enjoy activities, go for a shopping spree, exploring tourist attraction and more.

Architecture that will overwhelm the artsy you! (It's that amazing!): Architecture is a muse, it is like fastening the seat belt on a time machine and going as far as one can go. Of all the things that define the diversity of India, architecture has been one aspect standing out always. The history right from the Indus Valley has been spiraled around town planning which paved a way to the emergence of Buddhism in India. The magnanimous era of caves, monasteries, and stupas began from here and lead to some of the best architectural styles present in India. Not to forget India's history of two millennia which has seen a number of global influences in the form of architecture. That being said, the country is majorly known for its 37 UNESCO World Heritage Sites and all the other marvels. From Taj Mahal, Ajanta Caves, Hill Forts of Rajasthan, Golden Temple, Sanchi Stupa to Group of Monuments at Mahabalipuram, the list of astounding intricacy stretches far wide. This is to say that you might run out of days on your vacation to India, but not of phenomenal architecture.

Food that makes its way to your heart: The sizzle of butter, aroma of spices, the texture of the cream, is your stomach growling already? Wait till you try the vivid cuisines of India. The nation is a melting pot of not only culture but a thousand flavors. Come to 'Dilwalon ki Dilli' fairly meaning the city of loving people, or 'Aamchi Mumbai', Our Mumbai, and surprise your palette with street food. Enter Rajasthan to the beats of 'Padharo Mhare Desh and relish the famous Dal baati and finish with the sugary sweet Malpua. Taking it from here, meals and snacks in Gujarat are all about sugar, tomatoes, and lemon. The variety goes beyond words when it comes to Bengali food, Kashmiri food, Mughlai cuisine and there is no comparison for South Indian cuisine. The cuisines of India is synonymous to infinite zesty dishes. How many have you tried yet?
